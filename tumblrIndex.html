AXION is a unique, app-based interactive documentary exploring the Scientific Method and human metamorphosis by inviting a player to immerse themselves in a unique audiovisual world and reflect on their journey. For more information, visit axion.is

This tumblr is where we will post updates throughout the development process and announce upcoming screenings and demos.
